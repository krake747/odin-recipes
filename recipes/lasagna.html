Joshua Weissman's Lasagna Recipe

Description

A savoury homemade lasagna assembled and baked according to Joshua Weissman's instructions.

All ingredients are freshly bought or even made, such as the bechamel sauce or the lasagna pasta sheets.

Ingredients

  • 450g ground beef (chuck)
  • 450g ground beef (pork shoulder)
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 rib of celery
  • 2 medium sized onions
  • 60g tomato paste
  • 250ml white whine
  • 350ml chicken stock
  • 500ml crushed tomatoes
  • pancetta or guanciale
  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Parmigiano reggiano
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Make sauce by mixing the ground beef and pork in a bowl.
  2. Add oil over medium-high heat to large pot.
  3. Add meat mixture to pot and sear for 2-3 minutes. Remove from pan and set aside.
  4. Add guanciale or pancetta and fry. Once done add 1 diced onion, 1 diced celery and 2 diced carrots.
  5. Then add the tomato paste, white wine, the chicken stock, the crushed tomatoes and the meat mixture.
  6. Assemble the lasagna.
