Fired Rice

Description

Easy and quick fried rice dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of cooked rice
  • 3 eggs, scrambled
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 green onion, sliced thinly
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • Salt
  • 1 Tbsp (14 g) Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Tbsp (14 g) Granulated Sugar
  • 3 Tbsp (42 g) Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp (12 g) White Vinegar
  • 3 Cloves Garlic, grated
  • 1 Tbsp Ginger, grated

Steps

  1. Add rice to a rice cooker and let it cook. (Alternatively used leftover cooked rice)
  2. Make scrambled eggs in a non-stick skillet.
  3. In a small bowl, add granulated sugar, soy sauce, white vinegar and whisk together. Add grated garlic and ginger. Whisk together.
  4. In a medium-sized skillet add oil on high heat and then sti-fry frozen peas and carrots.
  5. Add rice, green onion and sauce
